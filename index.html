Open Access · Gujarati Mental Health

A centralized repository for Gujarati mental health resources

GujPsych Repository compiles psychometric scales, translated instruments, and awareness materials available in the Gujarati language for use in clinical practice and research. All resources are community-contributed and openly accessible.

Browse Scales Contribute a Resource

What is this repository?

A centralized open-access database of psychometric scales, clinical assessment tools, and mental health awareness materials that have been translated, adapted, or validated in the Gujarati language.

Who is it for?

Psychiatrists, psychologists, researchers, postgraduate students, and clinicians working with Gujarati-speaking populations in clinical or research settings.

How are resources added?

Any researcher, clinician, or student may submit a resource via the submission form. Submissions require documentation — a published paper, thesis, or IEC approval number.

Founding reference

This repository was established alongside a scoping review of Gujarati psychometric scales. The review identified 55 instruments and highlighted the need for a centralized accessible resource.
